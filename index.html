About

Kai Carstensen has held the Chair in Econometrics at Kiel University since 2014. He is also an External Research Professor at the ifo Institute and a member of the Scientific Advisory Council of the Kiel Institute for the World Economy.

He previously served as Dean of the Faculty of Business, Economics and Social Sciences from 2020 to 2022, Vice Dean from 2022 to 2024, and Senator of Kiel University from 2022 to 2024.

Research Focus

  • Empirical macroeconomics
  • Business cycle analysis
  • Business surveys and firm expectations
  • Forecasting methodology

Selected Background

From 2007 to 2014, Kai Carstensen was Professor of Economics at Ludwig Maximilians University Munich and Head of the Business Cycle Analysis and Surveys Department at the ifo Institute. In that role he coordinated the business surveys underlying the ifo Business Climate indicator and represented ifo in the Joint Economic Forecast for the German Federal Government.
