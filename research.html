Research

My research is concerned with empirical macroeconomics, business cycle indicators, survey expectations, uncertainty, and forecasting. Currently, a focus is the use of firm-level survey information to understand the dynamics of firm-level belief formation. I also have ongoing work on inflation and business cycle forecasting.
